About

About Me!

I work at the intersection of AI, product, and strategy — focusing on how intelligent systems can be applied to solve real-world business problems in a structured and meaningful way.

I made this website because I find it genuinely exciting to use technology to improve how things work. AI, in particular, feels like a turning point — it's made capabilities like building, learning, and experimenting far more accessible than they’ve ever been. I could never have imagined building something like this independently a few years ago, and now it feels like just the starting point.

What excites me most is the ability to take a problem I see around me and actually try to solve it — end-to-end. This platform is a space for me to experiment, think through ideas, and build towards that.

How I Think

I’m naturally drawn to complex, ambiguous problems — especially the kind that don’t have a clear starting point. My approach is usually to first understand the system: how decisions are made, what information is missing, and a clear focus on what is the end-user's largest pain point.

From there, I focus on structuring the problem, exploring multiple solution paths, and iterating until something practical emerges. I absolutely do not know when to quit, if I believe a problem needs to be solved, I'll use every resource and tool at my disposal to understand the context, the problem statement, devise a methodology, and iterate until it's solved in an effective and scalable manner.

Working across different industries, geographies, and technologies has made me comfortable navigating unfamiliar contexts and building clarity from ambiguity. My background at IIT Bombay has also shaped how I approach technical problem-solving, allowing me to connect business context with how systems actually need to be designed and executed.

What I Care About

I care about building things that are actually useful to society, not just technically interesting, but something that genuinely resolves major pain-points and improves how people live and work.

On a more personal level, I’m someone who’s always trying to stay optimistic and constructive. I like engaging with different perspectives, learning from people, and working towards things that have a meaningful, positive impact.

Beyond Work

Outside of work, I enjoy analysing films, reading (currently a lot of Dostoyevsky and philosophy), and exploring storytelling across formats. I love exposing myself to different environments and people of different cultures, and so I try to travel whenever I get the chance.

I’m also into anime and manga, which is honestly where a lot of my energy and curiosity comes from, along with quieter hobbies like crochet. I actively try to stay involved in social work whenever I can.
