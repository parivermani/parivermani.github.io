About Me!

I work at the intersection of AI, product, and strategy, focusing on how technology can be used to achieve business needs.

I made this website because I find it genuinely exciting to use technology to improve how things work. AI especially feels like a turning point. It has made building, learning, and experimenting far more accessible than before. A few years ago, I would not have imagined building something like this independently, and now it feels like the starting point.

What excites me most is taking a problem I see around me and trying to solve it end to end. This space is where I experiment, think through ideas, and build towards that.

How I Think

I am drawn to complex, ambiguous problems, especially the kind that do not have a clear starting point. I usually begin by understanding how the system works, how decisions are made, what information is missing, and what the end user actually struggles with.

Working across different industries, geographies, and technologies has made me comfortable navigating unfamiliar contexts and building clarity from ambiguity. My background at IIT Bombay has also shaped how I approach technical problem-solving, allowing me to connect business context with how systems actually need to be designed and executed.

What I Care About

I care about building things that are actually useful to society, not just technically interesting, but something that genuinely resolves major pain-points and improves how people live and work.

On a more personal level, I’m someone who’s always trying to stay optimistic and constructive. I like engaging with different perspectives, learning from people, and working towards things that have a meaningful, positive impact.

Beyond Work

Outside of work, I enjoy analysing films, reading (currently a lot of Dostoyevsky and philosophy), and exploring storytelling across formats. I love exposing myself to different environments and people of different cultures, and so I try to travel whenever I get the chance.

I’m also into anime and manga, which is honestly where a lot of my energy and curiosity comes from, along with quieter hobbies like crochet. I actively try to stay involved in social work whenever I can.
