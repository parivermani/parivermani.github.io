About

AI Systems • Product Thinking • Building Useful Things

I’m interested in the space where people, systems, and technology meet, especially when something complicated can be made clearer, lighter, or more useful.

My work has moved across consulting, strategy, delivery, and AI enablement, which has taught me how to turn ambiguity into something structured enough to act on. What I enjoy most is not just solving problems, but figuring out which problems are worth solving, and how to make the solution practical enough to use and scale.

I like learning new things, working with people, and translating ideas across technical and non-technical contexts. That combination has shaped how I work, from engineering at IIT Bombay to project work at Accenture, and it still guides the kinds of things I choose to build.

I hold a B.Tech from IIT Bombay, where I developed both a systems mindset and a habit of staying curious.
